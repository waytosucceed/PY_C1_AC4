Why is it Called Python?

Do you know why it has been named Python?

When its creator, Guido Van Rossum, started developing this language, he wanted to name it something short, unique, and slightly mysterious. He was reading the scripts from the TV show "Monty Python's Flying Circus" at the time and thought the name "Python" was a good fit for his new programming language.

That’s how it got its name!
